<p><h4><b>Purpose and legal basis of the processing</b></h4></p>
<p>Personal data are processed in order to allow registration to the platform and authentication of subjects making an electronic payment.<br/>The access logs to the site wisp2.pagopa.gov.it/wallet/ are treated in order to guarantee the security of the system and of the relative transactions, as well as for the execution of a task of public interest and connected to the exercise of public powers.<br/>The holder guarantees that no form of automated decision-making process is foreseen which involves legal effects on the interested party.</p>

<p><h4><b>Rights of the interested parties</b></h4></p>
<p>The interested parties have the right to obtain access to their personal data from the holder, correction or cancellation of the same, the limitation of treatment and the right to oppose the same as well as the right to the portability of personal data. Furthermore, the interested parties have the right to withdraw their consent at any time, without prejudice to the lawfulness of the treatment based on the consent given prior to the revocation. In the absence of consent or the provision of personal data required for authentication, however, it will not be possible to register with the platform and make electronic payments. <br/>Requests are submitted to the Agency by contacting the Data Protection Officer at the addresses indicated above.<br/>The interested parties, who consider that the processing of their personal data takes place in violation of the provisions of the Rules, have the right to propose a complaint to the Guarantor for the protection of personal data or to take appropriate judicial offices.</p>
